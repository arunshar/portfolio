I build physics-informed AI systems that make spatial reasoning reliable for real-world deployment: detecting GPS spoofing for safer autonomous navigation, modeling energy-efficient vehicle routing, and downscaling climate data for coastal flood-risk assessment. My research fuses deep generative models, diffusion models, transformers, physics-informed learning, and geostatistical priors to address data incompleteness, distribution shift, and violations of physical laws in spatial machine learning.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, advised by Prof. Shashi Shekhar, with committee members Prof. Vipin Kumar, Prof. Ravi Janardan, and Prof. Ying Song. My dissertation is Distortion-Aware Spatial Data Science (Doctoral Dissertation Fellowship, 2022-2023). At Esri, I shipped a maritime anomaly-detection pipeline on AWS that improved classification accuracy from 55% to 73% and reduced API latency by 30%.

Actively seeking Postdoc, Machine Learning Engineer, and Research Scientist roles.

Selected Publications

Geo-lucid Conditional Diffusion Models for High Physical Fidelity Trajectory Generation

Generative Modeling / Trajectory Forecasting / Urban AI

GCDM, a two-stage cascaded diffusion model that injects attributed road-network priors through map-informed latent variables, spatial cross-attention, and a physics-based trajectory decomposition; stronger geo-distribution similarity and velocity, acceleration, and jerk fidelity than DiffTraj, Sashimi, DiffWave, and EETG on Porto and Harbin taxi data.

Multi-Level Bi-Objective Routing for Pareto-Optimal Energy and Travel-Time Planning

Graph Algorithms / Route Optimization / Electric Vehicles

MBOR with a boundary multigraph, Multi-level Encoded Pareto Frontier View, 2D cost-interval pruning, and multi-edge pruning; retrieves complete Pareto-optimal path sets over fragmented road networks with more than 10x online-runtime improvement on real-world road data.

Physics-Based Trajectory Gap Detection and Possible Rendezvous Search

Trajectory Mining / Maritime Safety / Anomaly Detection

Space-Time Aware Gap Detection that reduces manual investigation areas for illegal maritime activity by up to 80%, combining comparison-less temporal indexing, R*-tree spatial indexing, maximal-union merge over space-time prism geo-ellipses, bi-directional pruning, and early stopping. The STAGD, TGARD, and DC-TGARD line scales to roughly 500K ships.

Hybrid Spatial Neural Surrogate Models for Sustainable Agriculture Simulation

Surrogate Modeling / Spatial ML / Sustainable Agriculture

SM-Hybrid, a surrogate for Daycent that models spatial autocorrelation and long-range tele-connections with hybrid spatial neural networks; predicts land emissions, ecosystem fluxes, and crop yield across Iowa, Illinois, and Indiana about 2x faster than Daycent while improving on SM-ANN baselines.

Spatially-Lucid AI Classification for Non-Euclidean MxIF Oncology Data

Biomedical AI / Spatial Deep Learning / Domain Adaptation

Domain-adapted spatially-lucid neural networks for multi-category point-set classification in non-Euclidean space, using spatial ensemble learning, place-calibration, weighted distance learning rates, spatial domain adaptation, mix-up masking, and contrastive predictive coding on MxIF oncology data.

Community-Engaged Mobility Analysis for COVID-19 Policy Response

GIScience / Mobility Analytics / Public Policy

An entity-relationship model, system architecture, and implementation for analyzing aggregated mobile-device data during COVID-19 policy interventions, delivering county-level analytics and fine-resolution device maps with the Minnesota Department of Management and Budget.
